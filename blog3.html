This was a week of flexing for web development where we delved into the world of wow we hate formatting. But our ancestors of the keyboard before us came up with these nifty things called flexboxes that saved many a web developer that came after them. They allow fun grouping and formatting of content much more user friendly than just trying to achieve the same level in CSS alone.

They allow the ability for expansion of windows, centering and distributing elements, formatting of all children within a parent element and much more.
